What is a flower?

A flower is the main reproductive part of the plant. A flower is also known as a bloom or blossom.

Here is a list of the different types of flowers.

  1. Lotus
  2. Lilies
  3. Iris
  4. Magnolia
  5. Jasmine
  6. Amaryllis
  7. Violet
  8. Lavender
  9. Orchid
  10. Tulip

The construction of a flower.

A Flowers construction.

Sepals
- Protects the developing flower bud during budding stage.
Petals
- Attracts pollinators like bees, butterflies to aid in pollination.
Stamens
- Male reproductive part of the flower.
Carpel
- Female reproductive part of the flower.
